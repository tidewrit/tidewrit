Pricing questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

What if my budget is unrealistic for my destination?

We tell you before you pay for a full plan. If your budget and destination don't match, we'll say so and suggest either a different destination or a different trip length. That honesty is the product.

Do you charge per traveler?

No. Pricing is per trip, whether it's two of you or a group of eight. Larger groups fit best in The Concierge tier because of the coordination involved.

What if I need the plan faster?

Rush delivery is available if your dates are close. Just mention it in your request and we'll quote it with your scope confirmation.

How do I pay?

Prices are shown in USD for clarity, and you pay the UGX equivalent at the day's rate via MTN or Airtel mobile money, or by card if you prefer. The exact amount is shown before you confirm.

When do I actually pay?

Right after you submit your request: we confirm scope and send a payment link by email and WhatsApp. Research starts once payment confirms, and your plan arrives within 3–5 days. If we spot that your budget and destination don't match, we tell you and refund or adjust before doing anything else.
