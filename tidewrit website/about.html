About Tidewrit

Built because listing photos lie.

Tidewrit plans every kind of escape: staycations, baecations, getaways and once-in-a-lifetime trips. All with the same rule: nothing unverified reaches your plan.

Honeymoons. Anniversaries. The birthday with a zero at the end. These trips carry weight that a regular vacation doesn't, and yet most people plan them the same way: twenty browser tabs, a listing with suspiciously perfect photos, and a budget that turns out to be missing the visa fee, the resort tax, and the airport transfer.

We started Tidewrit to do that work properly. Every plan is researched for your actual dates and budget. Every stay we recommend is checked against photos real guests posted recently, and anything that doesn't hold up gets excluded, with the reason written into your plan.

We're based in Kampala and we plan the whole planet. Everything we do - knowledge, paperwork, bookings, vetting, 24/7 support - travels at the speed of the internet, so it doesn't matter whether you're an American couple heading to Japan, a German family bound for Peru, or a Ugandan honeymoon in Fiji. Our time zone overlaps Europe, Asia and the Americas' mornings, which means answers while you sleep.

We're small and honest about it. A human confirms your trip's scope before you pay anything, tells you if your budget won't work, and answers on WhatsApp when your plans change mid-trip. As Tidewrit grows, more of this gets faster and smarter, but the verification promise stays the same.

The Tidewrit promise

No recommendation reaches your plan unchecked. No budget hides its ugly parts. No payment before scope is confirmed. If we wouldn't book it for our own celebration, it doesn't make your plan.
